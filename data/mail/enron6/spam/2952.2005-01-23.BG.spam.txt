wireless rebooting
nighthawk has designed simple , easy to install , wireless remote rebooting devices specifically for computer users , it and isp professionals . anyone who has ever experienced the frustration of having to travel to a remote site simply to reboot a locked - up computer , router , or other electronically controlled device prone to system lock - up will appreciate the benefit of this simple , ‘ plug and play ’ solution .
wireless traffic control
nighthawk systems have been used extensively in traffic applications all over the us . the low cost , wide coverage , and group call capability of paging make our controls the ideal solution for remote control of traffic applications of all types . paging will always outperform cellular in up front cost , monthly air time cost , geographic coverage , and the ability to address multiple signs with one call .
wireless firehouse alert systems
nighthawk systems continues to provide a great low cost solution for firehouse automation . using paging signals , our firehouse pt / fas can turn on lights , open doors , activate wake up alarms , activate firehouse traffic lights , and send messages to printers or variable message signs .
wireless electric utility applications
nighthawk has devoted a significant amount of research and development efforts specifically for utility industry applications . while our products can be used to remotely control virtually any electrical on \ off application , we have focused our development efforts in two areas that have short term pay back for utilities ; whole house disconnect and load management .

* * * * * * * * * * * * *
satellite services
nighthawk systems offers remote control services using the orbcomm network of low earth orbit satellites . this allows for reliable two way communications anywhere on earth . a user can activate controls from simple web page provided by nighthawk and receive confirmation back that the command was executed . the control device can also initiate an alarm and notify the user of a specific condition .
nighthawk systems , inc . and orbcomm have teamed up to provide superior service at very affordable rates .
