belgian energy group tractebel has won a large electricity contract in
the us . tractebel will manage all electricity acquisitions of us
electricity distributor niagara mohawk corporation , which has a customer
base of 1 . 5 million in the north of new york state . the value of the
contract has not been revealed .
temi , tractebel ' s us electricity trading and sales division , will
supply 35 , 000 gigawatt hours of electricity to the us distributor . in
comparison : total belgian electricity usage amounted to 75 , 000 gigawatt
hours in the year 2000 . niagara ' s customer base includes both private
and industrial users . temi is taking over all current contracts for
electricity supply to niagara .
